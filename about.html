About Tony Wolf

Independent investigative journalism serving the public interest
TonyWolf.press

Tony Wolf is an independent investigative journalism publication dedicated to in-depth reporting on the issues that matter most to the public interest. Our work focuses on government accountability, corporate power, public health, environmental justice, civil liberties, and global security.

We believe that rigorous, evidence-based journalism is essential to a functioning democracy. Every investigation we publish is grounded in documented facts, sourced from official records, peer-reviewed research, and on-the-ground reporting. We do not publish opinion disguised as news. We do not accept funding from corporations, political parties, or government entities. Our only obligation is to the truth and to the readers who depend on it.

Our Mission

The mission of Tony Wolf is simple: follow the facts wherever they lead, and present them clearly and completely to the people who need them most. In an era of information overload, algorithmic amplification of misinformation, and declining trust in institutions, the role of independent journalism has never been more critical. We aim to produce reporting that is not merely informative but consequential -- work that illuminates problems, holds power accountable, and empowers citizens with the knowledge they need to participate meaningfully in democratic life.

We specialize in long-form investigative reporting that takes the time to understand complex issues in their full context. Our articles are typically several thousand words in length because the problems we cover -- lead poisoning, disinformation campaigns, prison profiteering, surveillance overreach -- cannot be honestly addressed in a few hundred words. We prioritize depth over speed, accuracy over sensationalism, and substance over spectacle.

Our Standards

Every article published under the Tony Wolf byline adheres to the following standards:

Sourcing: All factual claims are attributed to identified sources, whether official documents, named experts, published research, or on-the-record statements. We do not rely on anonymous claims without corroboration.

Transparency: Our sources sections list every source cited in an article, with enough detail for readers to verify our reporting independently. When we make analytical judgments, we distinguish them clearly from reported facts.

Independence: Tony Wolf operates without advertising revenue, corporate sponsorship, or political funding. We are not beholden to any interest other than the public's right to know.

Corrections: When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently. If you find a factual error in our reporting, please contact us and we will investigate and correct as needed.

Topics We Cover

Our reporting focuses on several core areas:

Public Health: Environmental contamination, healthcare access, pharmaceutical industry practices, and the intersection of policy and public health outcomes.

Government Accountability: Federal, state, and local government actions, spending, regulatory enforcement, and the consequences of policy decisions on ordinary citizens.

Civil Liberties: Surveillance, privacy, free speech, criminal justice reform, and the balance between security and freedom.

Corporate Power: Industry influence on policy, antitrust enforcement, labor practices, and the social consequences of concentrated economic power.

Global Security: Disinformation campaigns, intelligence operations, geopolitical conflicts, and the information warfare shaping public understanding of world events.

Economic Justice: Taxation policy, wealth inequality, housing, infrastructure, and the structural factors that determine economic opportunity.

Contact

We welcome tips, corrections, and feedback from our readers. If you have information about a story we should investigate, or if you have found an error in our reporting, please reach out. Tony Wolf exists to serve the public interest, and the public's engagement is essential to that mission.

You can reach us through the contact information provided on our website, or by navigating to any of our published investigations and using the share and feedback tools provided there.
